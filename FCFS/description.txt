Algorithm FCFS
FCFS (First-Come, First-Served) is a scheduling algorithm that operates based on the order in which processes enter the system.
In this algorithm, the process that arrives first in the system is given higher priority in execution and continues until its completion.
As a result, if a process with a long execution time enters the system, subsequent processes have to wait for its completion.
